Campaign Statement

Voluntary commitments on AI are insufficient, for a technology that Nobel Prize winners, and even CEOs acknowledge poses an extinction risk to humanity at the same scale as pandemics and nuclear wars.

Most narrow AI applications are beneficials, from cancer scanning to protein development.

General AI systems pose extreme risks that countries around the world have committed to mitigating: we must move from voluntary commitments to regulatory oversight, no less than what we expect for safety critical industries like civil, aviation, and nuclear.
